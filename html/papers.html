ACQUIRE 2026

The 1st International Workshop on AI Code QUality, Integrity & REliability

Tue 7 April 2026, Canterbury, UK

Submission Guidelines

Submissions will be handled via EasyChair.

Submissions will undergo a single-blind review process. Authors are allowed to include their names and affiliations in the submitted manuscript.

Accepted papers will be submitted for publication to IEEE Xplore in a companion volume (EDCC-C). It is anticipated that the conference proceedings will be managed by the Conference Publishing Services (CPS) and submitted to Xplore and the CSDL.

Important Dates (AoE)

  • Paper submission Deadline: 19th January, 2026
  • Author Notification: 24th February, 2026
  • Camera-ready: 5th March, 2026

Call for Papers

Submission Types & Format

  • Full papers: 6–8 pages (references included), PDF, IEEE CPS format.
  • Short / position papers: 2–4 pages (references included), PDF, IEEE CPS format.
