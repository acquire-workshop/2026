Call for Papers

Submission Types & Format

We invite submissions in the following categories:

  • Research Papers (up to 8 pages, including references): Original, high-quality research contributions addressing theoretical, empirical, or methodological aspects at the intersection of AI and Software Engineering.
  • Short Papers (up to 4 pages, including references): Preliminary results, focused case studies, or well-defined contributions that are smaller in scope but offer valuable insights.
  • Dataset & Tool Papers (up to 4 pages, including references): Descriptions of datasets, benchmarks, or tools that enable reproducible research and innovation. Submissions must include sufficient detail for reuse, and links to public repositories are strongly encouraged.
  • Position Papers / New Ideas (up to 4 pages, including references): Visionary or thought-provoking ideas, early-stage research, or emerging challenges and opportunities for dependable, high-quality AI code generation.

Important: Please clearly indicate the type of your submission (e.g., Research Paper, Short Paper, etc.) on the first page of the manuscript.

Submission Guidelines

Submissions will be handled via EasyChair. All submissions must be written in English and adhere to the IEEE conference two-column formatting guidelines. Papers must be submitted in PDF format.

Submissions that exceed the page limits, are off-topic, or not formatted correctly will be rejected without review.

Submissions will undergo a single-blind review process. Authors are allowed to include their names and affiliations in the submitted manuscript. All accepted papers will be peer-reviewed by members of the program committee.

Accepted papers will be submitted for publication to IEEE Xplore in a companion volume (EDCC-C). It is anticipated that the conference proceedings will be managed by the Conference Publishing Services (CPS) and submitted to Xplore and the CSDL.

Authors of accepted papers will be invited to present their work at the workshop. At least one author of each accepted paper must register via the EDCC 2026 conference website and present the paper.
